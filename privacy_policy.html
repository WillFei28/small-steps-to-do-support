Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to the Small Steps To-Do application (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Small Steps Development (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is provided "AS IS".


What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?
What information do users provide to the Application?

The Application acquires the information you supply when you download and register the Application. Registration with the Service Provider is required to use the core features.

The information provided by users is categorized as follows:

  • Authentication Data: Information required for login, including your email address, password hash, or unique user identifiers (UID) provided by third-party authentication services (e.g., Firebase Authentication). This data is used to secure your account and manage your access.
  • Task Data: Data directly supplied by you for the core service, including your written goals, task input, generated step-by-step plans, suggested time durations, and completion status. This data is essential for the AI-powered task breakdown functionality.
  • Account Status Data: Information related to your usage limits, such as your subscription status, tracking of the daily input limit, and the current number of active tasks, which is used to enforce the Freemium model.
  • Contact Data: Information you provide when you contact the Service Provider via email for support or inquiries.

The Service Provider uses this information solely to provide the core services, manage accounts, and administer support.


What information does the Application collect automatically?

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's unique device ID, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.


Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.


Does the Application use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies?

The Application uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to enhance user experience and provide certain features. The AI components process user Task Data in the following ways:

  • Automated Functionalities: User-provided goals are sent to an external AI model (Google Cloud AI/Gemini AI) to be instantly broken down into a structured, manageable list of subtasks.
  • Service Provision: AI processing is strictly used to deliver the core task breakdown functionality and is performed in accordance with this privacy policy.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

The Service Provider shares your information with the following trusted third-party service providers solely to perform core application functions on our behalf. These providers do not have an independent use of the information disclosed to them and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google): Used to handle user authentication and manage your unique user ID and login credentials.
  • Google Cloud AI (Gemini AI): User-provided Task Data is transmitted to this service for processing and generating the step-by-step breakdown.
  • Zeabur (Hosting and Backend): Used for hosting the application's server and the primary data storage (PostgreSQL database). Your Task Data, Authentication Data, and Account Status Data are stored securely on this platform.

The Service Provider may also disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • when they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
